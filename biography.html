Keshav Poudel: Aerospace Engineer & Space Enthusiast

Born in a small village in Bardiya, Nepal, Keshav Poudel's journey to becoming an aerospace engineer is a testament to his unwavering determination and passion. From a young age, Keshav was fascinated by the vast expanse of the sky and dreamed of one day becoming a pilot. Despite limited resources and opportunities in his rural hometown, he excelled academically, particularly in mathematics and sciences.

Keshav's exceptional aptitude for mathematics became evident early in his education. His teachers recognized his talent and encouraged him to pursue higher education in STEM fields specially aerospace, the one he always dreams pursuing. This support, combined with his own intrinsic motivation, propelled him forward on his academic journey.

"The sky is not the limit. It's just the beginning of what we can achieve when we combine passion with perseverance."

After completing his secondary education with outstanding results, Keshav took on a journey to study aerospace engineering at IOE Pulchowk Campus. Throughout his university years, he maintained his focus on his studies while also participating in various extracurricular activities. He was particularly active in sports, excelling in football, cricket, and swimming.

The majestic Himalayan mountains that inspired Keshav's love for exploration

Beyond his academic and athletic pursuits, Keshav developed a deep appreciation for nature and outdoor activities. He became an avid trekker and hiker, often exploring the beautiful landscapes of his home country, Nepal. These experiences further strengthened his connection to his roots and his love for Nepal.

As a technology enthusiast, Keshav stays at the forefront of innovations in aerospace engineering and related fields. His particular interest in space exploration has led him to participate in various research projects and conferences focused on advancing our understanding of the cosmos.

The wonders of space exploration that drive Keshav's research

Despite his achievements, Keshav remains humble and kind-hearted. He is known for his generosity and willingness to help others, regularly volunteering his time to mentor young students interested in STEM fields. His strong spiritual foundation guides his actions, and he believes in the importance of prayer and meditation in maintaining balance in life.

Today, Keshav continues to work toward his childhood dream of becoming a pilot while making significant contributions to the field of aerospace engineering. His story serves as an inspiration to young people from similar backgrounds, demonstrating that with determination, hard work, and passion, it is possible to overcome obstacles and pursue one's dreams.

Meditation has become an essential part of Keshav's daily routine
